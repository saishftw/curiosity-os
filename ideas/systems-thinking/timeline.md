# Timeline

## 2026-06-29 — Captured & first exploration

A ChatGPT session on process engineering became something much larger: a
reframing around universal systems thinking. The session started with the question
of how to learn process engineering and arrived at the insight that process
engineering, software architecture, and most analytical disciplines are all
specializations of the same underlying skill — modeling systems as transformations
under constraints. The exploration shifted the intent away from learning a field
and toward developing a mental framework that transfers across any domain.
