# Open loops

_Questions waiting to be explored — invitations, not tasks._

- What universal mental models recur across all engineered systems regardless of
  industry?
- What canonical frameworks do experienced process engineers use when analyzing
  unfamiliar organizations?
- How should visual models evolve from high-level conceptual diagrams to operational
  system maps?
- Which disciplines — Lean, Theory of Constraints, Operations Research, Systems
  Engineering — contribute the most transferable thinking patterns?
- Can a reusable "system interrogation framework" be formalized for any domain?
