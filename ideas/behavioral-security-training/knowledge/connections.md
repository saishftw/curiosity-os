# Connections

This Idea lives in the space _between_ several fields. That is the interesting part.

- **Psychology** — why people act against what they know.
- **Behavior design** — Fogg, habit loops, friction as a tool.
- **Learning** — spacing and retrieval beat one-off modules.
- **Design** — the intervention has to feel humane, not nagging.
- **Gamification** — handle with care: points can crowd out the real goal.
