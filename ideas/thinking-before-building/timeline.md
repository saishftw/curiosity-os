# Timeline

## 2026-06-28 — Captured

As a software engineer, the instinct to jump straight to implementation runs deep.
The moment a problem is described, the mind reaches for architecture. I want to
deliberately separate product thinking from engineering thinking — to spend real time
exploring the "why" and "what" before the "how". Captured this as both a personal
skill to develop and a framework that could influence how future ideas are evaluated.
