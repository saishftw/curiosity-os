# Market

## Who feels this pain

- Small teams shipping LLM features without a FinOps function.
- Agencies billing clients for AI usage they cannot itemize.

## Why now

Token prices fell, but usage rose faster. The bill became unpredictable right as it
became material.

## What exists today

- Generic cloud cost tools — blind to _why_ tokens were spent.
- Model gateways — focused on routing, not on eliminating redundant context.

The gap: **nobody explains the bill in the language of the conversation.**
